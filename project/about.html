About Backyard Hunting

Backyard Hunting started as a project idea for my BYU Pathway course WWD 131. It reflects the love I have for the outdoors. Many of the hobbies I have are based on this. I grew up in Big Sky country... for those that don't know, that would be Montana. I lived in an area already in the tops of the mountains. We had the Missouri River run through the middle of the city. It was right down the street from me (maybe 3 or 4 city blocks). Every summer I lived in that river. When I wasn't in the river or fishing in that river you would find me exploring outdoors. Anywhere my little legs or my dirt bike could take me I went. I also had a Honda Trail 70 that took me all over through the countryside.

I grew up with BB guns and a bow and arrow. I can't even count how many homemade bows and arrows I made, or the number of fishing poles. Some of them were horrible, but others worked well. I was also a boy scout. I loved camping, and I will never forget the weeklong backpacking trip we took in the Bob Marshall Wilderness. I have so many great memories of my scouting experiences.
